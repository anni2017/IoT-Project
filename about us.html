About SISTec

Sagar Institute of Science and Technology (SISTec) is a renowned engineering college located in Bhopal, established in 2007. It is part of the sagar group of institutions and offers B.tech, MBA, and M.tech programs. SISTec is known for its modern facilities, diverse student body, and accomplished faculty, making it a leading institution in providing quality education. The institution is committed to innovation in its curriculum and aims to contribute to the advancement of technical education in India, striving to become one of the top technical education providers in the country.

About IoT

The internet of things (IoT) is a revolutionary concept that has transformed the way we interact with and utilize technology. At its core, IoT refers to the extensive network established by connecting a diverse array of devices to a shared network. These devices can encompass anything from household appliances and wearable gadgets to industrial machinery and vehicles. The fundamental premise behind IoT is the seamless exchange of data between these devices, leading to enhanced functionality and efficiency in numerous aspects of our lives.
